About

I came up through healthcare IT — database ops, systems administration, the kind of work that teaches you how things actually break. That foundation made me a better GRC professional: I understand what I'm governing, not just the frameworks that say I should govern it.

At CareDx, a HIPAA-regulated SaaS company in the organ transplant space, I led a SOC 2 Type II program across 280+ controls. I built a third-party risk function that processed 300+ vendor assessments annually and wrote the policies that governed them. When customers sent security questionnaires — SIG, CAIQ, custom — I answered them with specificity, because I knew the controls firsthand.

I'm now at Rightway Healthcare, continuing to build compliance programs that scale.

Outside of work I maintain a home lab where I build Python security tools. It's where I turn framework knowledge into running code — and where the projects below came from.
